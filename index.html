The Problem

Which music fits best for an image or video? This is a subjective but highly relevant question for the media and ad industry. At LYBS, we developed an AI called Vision2Music that suggests music for any given image or video.

To our knowledge, the only comparable feature in the market is Soundmatch by Epidemic Sound. Their approach, as they describe it:

Soundmatch identifies what the shot in the video contains and generates relevant keywords for a semantic search. It then uses data insight into how these keywords are typically used at scale, powered by over 2.5 billion daily views of YouTube videos containing Epidemic Sound music. — Epidemic Sound

Two Architectures, One Goal

Soundmatch bridges the gap with keywords — mainly capturing the mood of an image. Our approach uses a direct neural connection between the two AIs, meaning they are not limited to communicating through keywords. This unlocks something powerful: figuration.

Figuration means the AI can musically represent specific objects and elements within an image — not just its overall mood. This leads to far more diverse, surprising, and contextually rich suggestions.

Examples

The following examples demonstrate how figuration allows Vision2Music to respond to different elements within the same image — while Soundmatch tends toward more uniform suggestions.

Example 01

Formula 1 — Monte Carlo

Vision2Music sees two distinct elements: the Monte Carlo villa in the background and the Formula 1 car. It responds with an opera track for the elegance and a high-energy EDM track for the speed. Soundmatch suggests mostly upbeat pop and EDM — capturing the energy, but not the duality.

Conclusion

Both approaches have their merits. Soundmatch leverages an enormous dataset and delivers reliable, broadly appealing results. Vision2Music, with its direct neural connection, trades that reliability for something more interesting: the ability to interpret an image, not just classify it.

For commercial, generic use cases, Soundmatch is the safe choice. But if you're looking for music that truly responds to what's in an image — with all its nuance, contradiction, and surprise — Vision2Music opens up possibilities that keywords alone cannot reach.
